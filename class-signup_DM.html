Mounted Weapons

Armored Longsword

$40/month. Proficiency with shor sword or Fencing required. Longswords and some weighted padding in leiu of armor provided. You are welcome to bring in your own armor, we will inspect it for appropriateness before allowing its use.

Martial Arts

$25/month. Absolutely no shoes allowed in the Dojo area. Purchase of judo-type gi required, available for purchase here. You will start as white belt regardless of your level from another Dojo. If you have prior experience, you can test after a month of white-belt training.

Yoga

$25/month. Yoga mats, blocks, and belts available for purchase. Padded Dojo area used so yoga mats may not be required, your choice. Absolutely no shoes allowed in the Dojo area.

Rock Climbing

$25/month. Small bouldering area and one top-roped route. We are NOT a rock gym. We only teach the basic safety and techniques. All personal equipment must be purchased elsewhere. Only ropes provided.

Dungeon Running

$100/month. Augmented reality is combined with weapons and a rotating obstacle course. Team of three to six required. Each month will be a new course. Proficience with at least TWO other courses required.

Middle Eastern Dance

$25/month. Learn the beautiful art of Middle Eastern Dance, from some of the most celebrated dancers in the region! Two permanent instructors, as well as guest instructors on a rotating basis. No costume required.
